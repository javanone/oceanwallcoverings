We provided Brian keys to our house and he worked independently, which was important for us since we do not reside full time in the home. He was also flexible in approaching the project as we expanded it to include additional rooms and treatments, and guided us in various decision points associated with the installations (including borders around complex fireplace mantles, quirky corners, and treatments involving air return vents). Brian also provided us with status reports during the course of the installation and contacted us as necessary with any questions he had. Brian worked cleanly and meticulously, and probably left our home cleaner than he found it.
